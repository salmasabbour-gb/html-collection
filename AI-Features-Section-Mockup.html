How customers earn points as they shop, designed to be easily communicated.

Redemption value

Tiers and thresholds

How customers move up tiers, set by whichever of spend or orders segments them most clearly.

How Adam works

From raw data to a launched campaign.

1

Identifies best-in-class

2

Creates segments

It groups your customers into meaningful segments based on their behavior and profile data.

3

Spots risks & opportunities

It compares each segment to your best customers to surface where revenue is slipping or waiting to grow, each with an estimated monthly impact.

4

Adam builds a ready-to-launch campaign for each one, the type, reward, and minimum order value configured for you. You approve and launch.
