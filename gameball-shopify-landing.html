The problem

Loyalty that works is hard to set up, and quietly leaks revenue once it's live.

Setting up loyalty that works takes expertise most merchants don't have, and can't afford to hire. Once it's live, two things go uncaught: customers quietly churning, and customers ready to spend more.

The leakage continues; the opportunity passes.
